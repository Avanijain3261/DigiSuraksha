Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Digi Suraksha?

+

Digi Suraksha is a platform dedicated to safeguarding users from online threats such as phishing scams, malware, and misinformation. We provide tools and resources to help users stay safe while browsing the internet.

2. How can I use Digi Suraksha to check a link?

+

Simply enter the URL you want to check into the input field on our homepage and click the "Check" button. Our system will analyze the link and provide you with information about its safety status.

3. Can Digi Suraksha guarantee 100% protection from online threats?

+

While Digi Suraksha employs advanced techniques to detect and flag potential threats, no system can guarantee complete protection. It's important for users to remain vigilant and adopt safe browsing practices to minimize their risk online.

4. Does Digi Suraksha offer any educational resources on cybersecurity?

+

Yes, Digi Suraksha provides educational resources, including blog posts, articles, and guides, to help users learn more about cybersecurity best practices and how to protect themselves online.

5. Is Digi Suraksha available in languages other than English?

+

At this time, Digi Suraksha is only available in English. However, we are working to expand our language support in the future to make our services accessible to a wider audience.
